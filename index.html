Pudding

When you are in the bad mood or glommy, would you like to have some sweet and appetizing dessert to make you happier? Pudding is suitable for you. It is delicious and delectable. A well done pudding will also melt and left such a pleasant aftertaste in your mouth. Actually, we can make it easily in our home. The ingredient is easy to be found and don't need a longer time to prepare for it.




Ingredient

Happy grass pudding powder, 3000ml water, 100g sugar, 1 can 400ml evaporated milk, some pandan leaves



Procedure

Step 1

Boiling 3000ml of water and put some pandan leaves into the water to make the pudding more fragant and smells good

Step 2

Add some sugar and pour some milk into it.

Step 3

Mix them prorperly and then turn off the fire.

Step 4

Add the pudding powder into the mixture and stir for 5 minutes.

Step 5

Get the fruit sugar from the packet and pour it into the mixture. Stir it properly.

Step 6

Pour the liquid pudding into the mould.

Step 7

Let the pudding cold down to the room temperature and put it into the refrigerator.
